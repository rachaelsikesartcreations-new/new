Build the first full draft of a marketing homepage for RepWallet. Desktop and mobile. This is a real first draft, build every section below in order.

=== PRODUCT CONTEXT ===
RepWallet is a credential management app for medical device sales reps. Before a rep is allowed into a hospital operating room they must be credentialed: vaccinations, HIPAA training, background checks, liability insurance, and more, spread across 4 to 5 fragmented platforms (symplr, GHX Vendormate, HealthTrust, ORtrax). RepWallet is one portable vault plus a live compliance dashboard with LifeLock-style monitoring. Real MVP features: credential dashboard with green/amber/red status, portable document vault with AI metadata extraction, AI document pre-screening, smart expiration alerts (60/30/7 day), one-tap platform access, and a document scanner. Audience: medical device reps first, hospital credentialing managers second, partnership and investor viewers third.

=== BRAND VOICE ===
Clinical, confident, trustworthy. Closer to medical-device or hospital software than a consumer app. Clear, not cute. The audience is sophisticated and time-pressed, so clarity beats flash.

=== DO NOT (anti-generic) ===
No generic blue SaaS template. No Linear-clone hero. No wall of equal drop-shadow cards. No logo wall. No two-tone split-color headline. No healthcare stock photography. Visual references for the bar to clear: Stripe (whitespace and product storytelling), Linear (typographic restraint), Okta (infrastructure confidence), CertifyOS and Athenahealth (healthcare credibility), Ramp (premium polish).

=== NAVIGATION / IA ===
Top nav: Home, Platform, For Reps, For Partners, Resources, Company. Right side: Log in, Request a Demo (primary button).
Footer: same nav expanded, plus Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, HIPAA Notice.

=== TYPOGRAPHY ===
Inter throughout (use "Semi Bold" with a space, not "SemiBold").
Hero headline 56 to 64px, Bold, line-height 1.05. Section headers 36 to 40px Semi Bold. Subheads 20 to 24px. Body 16 to 18px, line-height 1.6, Regular. Eyebrow labels 12 to 13px, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.08em.

=== COLOR (provisional, navy system) ===
Primary Navy #1F4E79 (anchors the majority of authority). Secondary Blue #32699A. Accent Sky Blue #60A9E9 (used sparingly, mostly for motion/hover/connection visuals). Background White #FFFFFF dominant. Do not overuse bright blue.
Status colors (the signature system): green #2E9E72 compliant, amber #E08A2B expiring soon, red #C0432F blocked. Use these as the visual language across hero cards, the workflow timeline, and the ecosystem.

=== GLOBAL LAYOUT ===
De-framed and whitespace-driven. Let space create separation, not boxes. Containers only where they carry meaning (the phone, an AI message). Vary the vertical rhythm: large breathing room after the hero, compress the supporting modules, large room again before the AI section. Mobile-first product, so the phone UI is the centerpiece. Background contrast flow: light hero, light workflow, soft neutral ecosystem, dark immersive AI section, light newsroom, dark final CTA.
